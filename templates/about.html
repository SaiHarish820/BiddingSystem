About our website

This website is a place where the price of a product or service is determined by the winning bid. The auction site allows the user to name their own price for any posted products or services within a certain time frame. The price rises depending on the bidding people.

Simple steps to be followed for creating or participating in a bid

for creating a new bid

  • go to home page
  • on the right side there will be button click on create bid
  • enter all the required information
  • you will get a notification if anyone wish to participate in your bid you will get the notification of the list of participants

to participate in a bid

  • go active bid page
  • you will get a table of bids
  • on the right you have an option participate in a bid
  • click on the button and provide the required information
  • if you are approved by the tenderer you will get a notification
  • you will get a notification if the tenderer approves your requirements
